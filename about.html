About

Learn about the idea of Material Design.

Material Design

Google designed "Material" with the notion of intuitiveness through simplicity. And through this simplicity, along with directed guidelines, Google envisions a unified ecosystem of design, from websites to mobile applications, so that the universal user can have a familiar, beautiful experience from all applications on all platforms.

Principles

Material is the metaphor

A metaphor of the underlying theory between space and motion. It is a combination between the basic materials of design, paper and ink, and the technological advancements in these pixels on a screen that allow imagination and magic happen with the touch of your fingers. This clash between familiar traits and a person's imagination helps users understand basic affordances, but also create new dimensions of usability.


Bold, graphic, intentional

Fundemental design properties such as typography, color, space, scale, grid, and much more are not just there to look pretty. It creates a hierarchy of layers within the screen, meaning in in different interatable mediums, and focus on the most imporant aspects within the program.


Motion provides meaning

Motion provides a relation between what they see in real life and on the screen. Great motion and movement on the screen reinforces the idea of the user as the prime mover and has complete control over the screen, but also understands the reasons for what is happening.
