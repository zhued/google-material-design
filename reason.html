Why Material Design?

So what is so special about Material Design huh?

There are so many designs out there in the computer and mobile phone world, some are great hits, others are misses. But what makes Material Design stand out of rest? Why should app developers implement Material Design into their products? Why should consumers be excited about Material Design?

Consistency

Within the modern world, we've all experienced inconsistency with design from different apps, as some apps look different on the phone than on your computer. It's caused a lot of frustration and extra time trying to get used to how the same programs functions on different platforms. Most applications such as mail, music, communications, and much more have complementary applications for tablet, smartphone, and computer. But if they don't get the design right for the different platforms and screen sizes, it will cause a lot of confusion for the users.

Material design principles bring in a guideline that allows unity between all platforms, so users can have a consistant, familiar layout with applications on whatever platform they choose.

Users First

Many designs focus too much on flashy and intricate animations or looks. But within the Material enviornment, everything is based around the user. From it's simplicity, to its meaningful tranistions, to even the smallest details in icons and pictures, the user's vision and understanding is the crucial point of interest.

Check out the side bars to see how different aspects of Material design cater to the universal users, and why some design principles were choosen over others!

Alive and Bright

Material is beautiful, vibrant, and alive. As seen throughout this site, Material is BRIGHT. It shines an exciting and energetic mood to the users and brings a spirited feel to the interactions with the users. It doesn't cut out any productivity either, as the simplicity will allow all users to enjoy and understand all aspects of the Material environment.

Check out the Experience tab in the sidebar to see an abundance of Material designed applications!

Face of Android

Android is in a very commanding position within the worldwide mobile platform, as it takes up more than 83% of the world-wide marketshare of mobile devices. Android was never known to be an aesthetically pleasing platform in the past, but with the introduction of Material Design coming to the newest Android 5.0 Lollipop version, the vision and design of Android will completely change for the better.

This will not only influence Android and Google, but it already has started to have an impact on many different designers and platforms to acquire some of Material Design's best features.
