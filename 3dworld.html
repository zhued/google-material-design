We live in a three dimensional world, where most solid objects are affixed and cannot pass through one another. Different arrangements of objects will allow our depth perceptions to identify distance and size. These attributes are second nature to us, has we see them every day, so Google applied them into material design.

Z Axis

Although a screen is only two dimensional and flat, the virtual z-axis is crucial to the user's visibility of familiarities between the physical world and the pixelated world.
